Search Engine Optimization - SEO Marketing

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is a collection of techniques and strategies aimed at improving a website's visibility and organic (non-paid) search engine rankings. By optimizing various aspects of a website, SEO helps drive targeted traffic, increase brand awareness, and improve overall user experience.

Keyword Research: SEO begins with extensive keyword research to identify the relevant search terms people use to find products, services, or information related to a website's content. This research helps prioritize target keywords for optimization.

What you will get under this service:

Keyword Research: SEO begins with extensive keyword research to identify the relevant search terms people use to find products, services, or information related to a website's content. This research helps prioritize target keywords for optimization.

On-Page Optimization: On-page optimization involves optimizing various elements within a website to make it search engine friendly. This includes optimizing meta tags, headings, URLs, images, and content to align with target keywords and improve relevancy.

Technical SEO: Technical SEO focuses on the backend aspects of a website that impact its visibility and crawling by search engines. This includes optimizing website speed, ensuring proper indexing, improving site architecture, fixing broken links, and implementing schema markup.
